# 🔥 Seasonal Synergy — User Manual

The **Seasonal Synergy** system tracks your long-term commitment to the clan. Earn points weekly to climb the seasonal rankings and earn the prestigious **Weekly MVP** title!

---

## 📈 Earning Synergy

Synergy is tracked in two ways: **Weekly** (short-term) and **Season** (long-term cumulative).

### 1. Weekly Energy Submission (`we`)
Submit your activity points during the weekend to contribute to your total:
- **Prefix**: `j we <points>`
- **Slash**: `/fs points:<number>`

### 2. Submission Rules (Core)
- **Weekend Only**: Regular members can only submit points on **Saturday and Sunday** (IST).
- **Daily Limit**: You can submit your points once per day during the weekend.
- **Season Carry-over**: Every point you submit to your Weekly Energy is automatically added to your total Season Synergy.

---

## 🏆 Weekly Reset & MVP Rewards

The leaderboard doesn't just track points—it rewards the best!

- **The Reset**: Every **Monday at 00:00 IST**, all Weekly Energy is reset to zero for the new week.
- **Weekly MVP Role**: The top 3 players on the leaderboard at the time of the reset are awarded the **Weekly MVP** role for the following week.
- **Achievements**: The #1 player each week earns a permanent "+1" to their **Weekly MVP Count** on their global profile.

---

## 📊 Leaderboard

The synergy channel features a visual leaderboard:
- **Stats**: Shows your Weekly Synergy (this week) and Season Synergy (total).
- **Visuals**: Displays your Discord avatar and current ranking.
- **Navigation**: Use the ◀ and ▶ buttons to browse the full clan rankings.

---

## 🛡️ Admin & Moderator Commands

Staff can manage energy levels and bypass restrictions:
- **Admin Submission**: `j we @user <points>` (Allows admins to submit for others on any day of the week).
- **Set Season Energy**: `j se @user <points>` (Directly overwrites a player's total season synergy).

---

> [!TIP]
> Don't forget to submit both on Saturday and Sunday to maximize your weekly total! Consistency is the key to winning the Season Championship.
