# 🛠️ Utility & Miscellaneous Plugins

This section covers the wide array of tools that handle everything from support tickets to voice recording and channel enforcement.

---

## 🎟️ [[Tickets]]
A standalone support ticket system.
- **Dynamic Creation**: Users can open tickets via buttons or commands.
- **Transcripts**: Automatically saves a full HTML transcript of the conversation upon closing.
- **Staff Routing**: Notifies the management role when a new ticket is opened.

---

## 👥 [[Teamup]]
A conversational matchmaking and group creation tool.
- **Lobby Management**: Create temporary lobbies for games or events.
- **Auto-Join**: Users can join teams by clicking buttons.
- **Reminders**: Pings participants when the team is full or the event is starting.

---

## 🎨 [[Celestial-HUD]]
A premium real-time status dashboard for members.
- **Embed HUD**: Displays live server stats and user-specific data in a high-end interface.
- **Auto-Update**: Refreshes hourly to ensure accuracy.

---

## 🧱 [[ChannelManagement]]
Enforces channel-specific rules to keep the server organized.
- **Media Only**: Automatically deletes text in media-only channels.
- **Link Detection**: Restricts links to designated channels.
- **Command Control**: Prevents bot commands from being run in general chat.

---

## ✉️ [[InviteTrackerAdvanced]]
Advanced invite monitoring with security features.
- **Raid Protection**: Detects and alerts staff of "Burst Joins."
- **Fake Detection**: Identifies users who join and leave immediately to farm invites.
- **Leaderboards**: Tracks which members bring in the most verified new users.

---

## 🛠️ [[Misc-Utilities]] (Say, Roles, Emoji, Sticker, Recorder)
Smaller tools that provide essential administrative functions:
- **Say**: Resend or edit messages through the bot by replying.
- **Roles**: Bulk role management and self-assignment tools.
- **Emoji/Sticker**: Steal and save assets from other servers into the global bank.
- **Recorder**: Multi-track voice recording for meetings or events.
