# 🛒 Market

The **Market** plugin handles all in-game currency exchanges, item listings, and social trading within the bot's ecosystem.

## 📋 Features

> [!INFO] **V2.0 Interface**
> The market has been recently upgraded to use individual, high-end embeds instead of simple text lists, improving readability on mobile.

- **Listing Management**: List items for currency or trade.
- **Auto-Sync**: Balances are verified via the `Economy` service before every transaction.
- **Pagination**: Browse thousands of items with smooth navigation.
